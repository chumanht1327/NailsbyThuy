Nails by Thuy — Private Nail Art Studio in Austin, TX

Nails by Thuy is a private, by-appointment nail art studio on Varrelman St in East Austin, TX 78725. Specialties include hand-painted freehand nail art, custom acrylic sets, Gel X, BIAB, dipping powder, and 3D nail art.

Location and Nearby Landmarks

The studio is located in the 78725 zip code of East Austin, Texas — conveniently close to Walter E. Long Metropolitan Park, Decker Lake, the Austin-Bergstrom International Airport (AUS), Highway 183, and the Highway 71 corridor. Clients travel from East Austin, Downtown Austin, Mueller, Manor TX, Del Valle, Pflugerville, Round Rock, Cedar Park, and Georgetown to visit the studio.

About the Nail Artist

Thuy (known as Twee) has been a licensed nail technician since 2011 — over 15 years of professional experience. She specializes in freehand nail art, meaning every design is hand-painted with brushes, not stamped or stickered. She is fluent in English and Vietnamese and serves Austin's diverse community.

Comparison: Private Studio vs Traditional Nail Salon

Unlike traditional nail salons in Austin that book multiple clients simultaneously, Nails by Thuy is a private one-on-one studio. There are no walk-ins, no waiting, no rushed appointments. Each client receives Thuy's complete attention for the full session duration. This results in higher quality, more detailed nail art and a significantly more relaxed experience.

Services Explained

BIAB (Builder-In-A-Bottle) is a gel-based strengthening treatment applied directly to natural nails — no extensions. Gel X are soft gel extensions applied using a dual-form technique for lightweight length. Acrylic full sets use liquid-and-powder acrylic for durable extensions. Dipping powder (SNS-style) uses a resin-and-powder system for odor-free, long-lasting color. All services can include freehand nail art add-ons.
