Freehand Nail Art

Thuy paints each set by hand with premium pigments and an artist’s eye for detail. This service is ideal if you want truly custom nail art that reflects your personality, mood, and style.

Highlights: hand-drawn florals, abstract texture, bespoke colour stories, 3D detailing, full custom concept work.

Best for: clients who want wearable nail art, statement designs, or a luxury salon experience with a private studio feel.

Acrylic Full Set

Premium acrylic full sets with a flawless finish and long-lasting structure. Shape, length, and design are customized to your vision, whether you prefer sleek elegance or bold accents.

Highlights: durable acrylic formula, polished finish, custom sculpting, tailored shape and length.

Best for: clients who want the classic strength of acrylic with a refined, artistic result.

Gel X Extensions

Gel X offers lightweight extension length without heavy bulk. This soft gel option delivers a natural feel with a polished look that is easy to wear and comfortable for active lives.

Highlights: soft-gel construction, natural movement, comfortable fit, strong finish.

Best for: clients who want longer nails without the weight of acrylics.

Builder Gel · BIAB

Builder Gel BIAB strengthens the natural nail with a glossy, protective overlay. This service is perfect for clients seeking durability, growth support, and a polished finish without extensions.

Highlights: nail strength, flexible coverage, shiny finish, natural nail enhancement.

Best for: clients who want stronger, longer natural nails with premium care.

Dipping Powder Nails

Dipping powder delivers a long-lasting manicure with deeply pigmented colour and excellent wear. This technique is ideal for clients who want a strong, low-odor finish that still feels lightweight and polished.

Highlights: vibrant colour depth, superior durability, no UV curing, smooth satin finish.

Best for: clients who want a dependable, high-performance manicure that holds up beautifully between appointments.
