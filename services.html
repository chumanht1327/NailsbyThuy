Builder Gel · BIAB

Builder-In-A-Bottle (BIAB) is a gel-based overlay applied directly to natural nails — no extensions. It adds flexible, durable coverage with a mirror-glossy finish that supports natural nail growth. BIAB is the ideal choice for clients who want stronger nails without extra length.

Common Questions

How long do Gel X nails last?
Gel X extensions typically last 3–5 weeks with proper aftercare — avoiding prolonged water exposure and using cuticle oil daily extends wear significantly.
What is the difference between BIAB and Gel X?
BIAB strengthens your natural nails with a gel overlay — no added length. Gel X adds a soft gel extension tip for length. Both can include freehand nail art. Full comparison →
Can I combine freehand art with any service?
Yes — freehand nail art can be added to any service. Whether you choose acrylic, Gel X, BIAB, or dipping powder, every set can include custom hand-painted designs.
