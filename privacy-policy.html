Privacy Policy

This website does not collect personal data, use cookies, or run analytics. Here's exactly what happens when you visit.

Effective: May 2026 · Nails by Thuy — Austin TX

What This Site Does Not Do

Nails by Thuy does not use cookies, browser storage, or any tracking scripts. There is no analytics platform (Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, etc.) installed on this site. No personal information is collected, stored, or sold.

Fonts

This site serves all fonts (Cormorant Garamond and DM Sans) directly from its own CDN. No font files are loaded from Google Fonts or any third-party server. Your browser does not contact fonts.googleapis.com or fonts.gstatic.com when visiting this site.

Google Reviews (Places API)

The homepage displays live reviews fetched from the Google Places API. This fetch happens on Netlify's servers — your browser never contacts Google directly for review data. No user data is sent to Google as part of this request. The response is cached for 24 hours so Google is contacted at most once per day, site-wide.

Booking via SMS or Phone

Clicking a booking link opens your device's default messaging or phone app. Any communication happens directly between you and Thuy through your carrier. This website does not transmit, store, or process any messages or call data.

Google Maps Embed

The services page includes an embedded Google Map. When you interact with the map, Google may set cookies or collect data according to Google's Privacy Policy.

Instagram Links

Links to @nailsbythuyh on Instagram open Instagram's website in a new tab. Once you leave this site, Instagram's own privacy policy applies.

Netlify Hosting

This site is hosted on Netlify. Netlify may collect standard server logs (IP addresses, timestamps, requested URLs) for operational purposes. See Netlify's Privacy Policy for details.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new effective date.
